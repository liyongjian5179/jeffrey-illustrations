```text
Jeffrey, a warm and focused young male AI builder and DevOps engineer, black or deep navy-black short rounded choppy hair with a compact cap-like silhouette, low rounded top close to the head, small uneven hair clumps, no visible hard part line, short bangs falling forward across the forehead above the eyebrows, simple rounded cute hand-drawn face, round glasses, no beard, deep blue over-ear headphones with warm yellow inner rings, Jeffrey Blue hoodie with a small yellow J logo on the left chest, jeans, white sneakers, accompanied by Byte, a small compact blue-yellow-cream AI robot assistant with blue screen face, dot eyes and yellow side accents. Palette lock: hoodie main blue #5379E8, headphone / outline accent blue #2F56C5, warm yellow accent #FFD84A, cream background #FFF9EE, denim jeans #88A8F5, hair / line dark #2B2F36. Warm hand-drawn look, subtle crayon / colored pencil texture mainly on characters, props and outlines, clean thick outlines, mostly flat clean cream background, high whitespace, clean readable labels and cards, cute but not childish, technical but not hacker style.
```

## Scene Prompt Template

```text
Create a 16:9 warm hand-drawn illustration for a Chinese technical article.

Character:
[Master Character Prompt]

Scene:
Jeffrey is [doing action] with Byte. The scene visualizes the idea: “[core idea]”.

Composition:
One clear metaphor, high whitespace, cream background, Jeffrey or Byte must perform the core action, no PPT-style diagram.

Labels:
Use at most 2-3 short Chinese handwritten labels, each 2-6 Chinese characters. Avoid UI panels full of text unless the user explicitly asks for an infographic.

Style:
Warm hand-drawn look, subtle crayon / colored pencil texture mainly on characters and props, clean thick outline, mostly flat clean cream background, clean readable labels, palette lock #5379E8 / #2F56C5 / #FFD84A / #FFF9EE / #88A8F5 / #2B2F36, not childish, not cyberpunk.
```
