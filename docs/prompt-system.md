# Prompt System

## Master Character Prompt

```text
Jeffrey, a warm and focused young male AI builder and DevOps engineer, black or deep navy-black short rounded choppy hair with a compact cap-like silhouette, small uneven hair clumps, soft off-center flow without a visible hard part line, short bangs above the eyebrows, simple cute hand-drawn face, round glasses, no beard, deep blue over-ear headphones with warm yellow inner rings, Jeffrey Blue hoodie with a small yellow J logo on the left chest, jeans, white sneakers, accompanied by Byte, a small compact blue-yellow-cream AI robot assistant with blue screen face, dot eyes and yellow side accents. Warm hand-drawn crayon and colored pencil texture, clean thick outlines, cream background, high whitespace, cute but not childish, technical but not hacker style.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
no beard, no mustache, no business suit, no cyberpunk, no dark hacker room, no realistic 3D, no semi-realistic portrait, no brown hair, no visible 4:6 part, no center part, no curtain bangs, no tall wavy quiff, no spiky crown, no mature handsome face, no anime big eyes, no long hair, no pompadour, no undercut, no black hoodie, no complex text, no crowded UI, no character design board, no faded alternative sketch thumbnails, no large floating orb robot, no over-detailed background
```

## Scene Prompt Template

```text
Create a 16:9 warm hand-drawn illustration for a Chinese technical article.

Character:
[Master Character Prompt]

Scene:
Jeffrey is [doing action] with Byte. The scene visualizes the idea: “[core idea]”.

Composition:
One clear metaphor, high whitespace, cream background, Jeffrey or Byte must perform the core action, no PPT-style diagram.

Labels:
Use at most 2-4 short Chinese handwritten labels, each 2-6 Chinese characters.

Style:
Warm crayon texture, clean thick outline, Jeffrey Blue and Warm Yellow brand colors, not childish, not cyberpunk.
```
