```text
Create a 16:9 warm hand-drawn illustration for a Chinese technical article.

Jeffrey is a warm young male AI builder and DevOps engineer. Keep him close to the Jeffrey reference IP: black / deep navy-black short rounded choppy hair with a compact cap-like silhouette, low rounded top close to the head, small uneven hair clumps, no visible hard part line, short bangs falling forward across the forehead above the eyebrows, simple rounded cute face, round glasses, no beard, deep blue over-ear headphones with yellow inner ring, Jeffrey Blue hoodie with small yellow J logo, jeans and white sneakers. Do not make him semi-realistic, older, brown-haired, adult-handsome, long-faced, oval-eyed, side-swept or spiky-haired.

Byte is a small compact blue-yellow-cream AI robot assistant with a cream body, blue screen face, simple dot eyes, yellow side accents, tiny arms / legs or stable side pods. Do not make Byte a large floating orb, drone, glossy robot head, or a body with a big "Byte" label.

Palette lock when no reference image is attached: hoodie main blue #5379E8, headphone / outline accent blue #2F56C5, warm yellow accent #FFD84A, cream background #FFF9EE, denim jeans #88A8F5, hair / line dark #2B2F36. Keep colors close to these values; avoid purple-blue, cyan, orange, gray backgrounds and brown hair.

Scene: [describe one clear action and metaphor].

Composition mode: [Warm Article Mode for WeChat/blog/article scenes, or Clean Skill Mode for README/docs/Skill examples].

Style: warm hand-drawn look, thick clean outlines, cream background #FFF9EE, high whitespace, Jeffrey blue-yellow brand palette, cute but not childish, technical but not cyberpunk. Use subtle crayon / colored pencil grain mainly on Jeffrey, Byte, clothing, props and outlines. Keep the cream background mostly flat and clean. Keep Chinese labels, cards and UI-like panels clean and readable, with only light hand-drawn outline texture.

Text: at most 2-4 short Chinese handwritten labels, each 2-6 Chinese characters.

Props: In Warm Article Mode, allow a few creator-context props only when they support the scene. In Clean Skill Mode, keep only objects required by the metaphor.

Avoid: business suit, beard, cyberpunk, dark hacker room, anime eyes, semi-realistic portrait, brown hair, purple hoodie, cyan headphones, orange logo, gray background, heavy full-image crayon grain, dirty paper texture, smudged text, noisy UI cards, visible 4:6 / center part, curtain bangs, side-swept adult bangs, tall wavy quiff, spiky crown, large exposed forehead, long oval eyes, narrow chin, mature face, 3D render, long text, crowded UI, character design board, many faded sketch thumbnails, decorative props that distract from the metaphor.
```
