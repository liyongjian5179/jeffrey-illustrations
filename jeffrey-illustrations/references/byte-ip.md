# Byte IP

Byte is Jeffrey's AI assistant.

## Appearance

- small round robot
- blue / yellow / cream palette
- simple dot eyes
- cream body, blue screen face, yellow side accents
- short antenna allowed
- small J or terminal mark allowed
- no complex mechanical details

## Role

Byte participates in the idea. It should hold, carry, point, remind, fix, celebrate or assist.

If Byte is in the scene but does nothing, remove it or give it a job.
