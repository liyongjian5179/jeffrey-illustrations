# Jeffrey IP

Jeffrey is the default character.

## Identity

A warm young male AI builder, DevOps engineer and knowledge creator who likes AI, Obsidian, HomeLab, Linux, Docker, MCP and writing.

## Appearance

- short textured side-part hair, soft 4:6 part
- round glasses
- deep blue headphones with yellow inner ring
- Jeffrey Blue hoodie with small yellow J logo
- jeans
- white sneakers
- no beard
- Original and Q proportions are both supported; do not invent a third body proportion

## Personality

- warm
- focused
- curious
- builder mindset
- lightly playful
- not hacker, not business elite

## Proportion

Two supported visual variants:

- Original: 3.8–4 heads tall, better for blog / README / banner.
- Q: around 3 heads tall, better for avatar / stickers / skill icons.

Both share the same hair, outfit, colors and logo.
