# Jeffrey IP

Jeffrey is the default character.

## Identity

A warm young male AI builder, DevOps engineer and knowledge creator who likes AI, Obsidian, HomeLab, Linux, Docker, MCP and writing.

## Appearance

- young, soft and approachable; not adult-handsome, not semi-realistic
- black or deep navy-black hair; never brown hair
- short rounded choppy hair, compact cap-like silhouette, small uneven hair clumps, soft off-center flow without a visible hard part line
- bangs fall forward in short clumps across the forehead
- top hair is low, rounded and close to the head; no raised crown or side-swept adult hair mass
- round glasses
- deep blue headphones with yellow inner ring
- Jeffrey Blue hoodie with small yellow J logo
- jeans
- white sneakers
- no beard
- Original and Q proportions are both supported; do not invent a third body proportion
- keep facial features simple: rounded head, small nose, simple mouth, round glasses, gentle round eyes; avoid long face, narrow chin, oval adult eyes, realistic cheek shading or mature facial structure

## Personality

- warm
- focused
- curious
- builder mindset
- lightly playful
- not hacker, not business elite

## Proportion

Two supported visual variants:

- Original: 3.8–4 heads tall, better for blog / README / banner. Keep the head large enough to feel like the reference, not a realistic adult body.
- Q: around 3 heads tall, better for avatar / stickers / skill icons.

Both share the same hair, outfit, colors and logo.

## Drift guardrails

Reject and regenerate when Jeffrey:

- looks like a generic anime / manga boy instead of the reference IP
- looks older, handsome, semi-realistic or influencer-like
- has brown hair, visible 4:6 / center part, curtain bangs, tall wavy quiff, spiky crown, side-swept adult bangs, wavy salon hair or realistic hair highlights
- has long oval eyes, narrow chin, large exposed forehead or adult face structure
- loses the yellow J logo, blue hoodie, round glasses or deep blue headphones
- appears as a character design poster with many surrounding sketch variants when the user requested an article / README illustration
